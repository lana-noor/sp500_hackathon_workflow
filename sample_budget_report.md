# Budget Variance Report — Q1 2026

**Organisation:** Emirates Digital Authority (EDA)
**Period:** January – March 2026
**Prepared by:** Office of Finance & Procurement
**Report Date:** 2026-03-27
**Currency:** AED
**Report Reference:** EDA-FIN-BVR-2026-Q1

---

## Executive Summary

Total Q1 2026 expenditure reached AED 21,470,000 against an approved budget of AED 19,450,000,
resulting in an overall variance of AED +2,020,000 (+10.4%). Two departments — Finance & Procurement
(+30.6%) and Information Technology (+15.5%) — exceeded their approved quarterly allocations by
material amounts requiring CFO-level review under EDA budget variance policy. Infrastructure &
Facilities also recorded a significant overspend of +14.7% driven by emergency capital works.
Three departments came in under budget, partially offsetting the total overrun.

## Expenditure Detail by Department

### Information Technology (IT)

| Item | Amount (AED) |
|---|---|
| Approved Q1 Budget | 4,200,000 |
| Actual Q1 Spend | 4,850,000 |
| Variance | +650,000 (+15.5%) |

**Breakdown of overspend:**

| Expenditure Line | Budgeted | Actual | Variance |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cybersecurity Licences (emergency procurement) | 0 | 400,000 | +400,000 |
| Cloud Infrastructure Scaling | 800,000 | 1,050,000 | +250,000 |
| Software Maintenance & Support | 1,200,000 | 1,200,000 | 0 |
| IT Staff Costs | 2,200,000 | 2,200,000 | 0 |

**Justification:** An unplanned ransomware threat in February 2026 necessitated emergency procurement
of endpoint detection and response (EDR) licences across all EDA endpoints (AED 400,000). Cloud
infrastructure scaling was required to support the launch of the National Digital Services Portal
earlier than planned. Both items have been formally documented and approved by the CIO.

---

### Human Resources (HR)

| Item | Amount (AED) |
|---|---|
| Approved Q1 Budget | 2,800,000 |
| Actual Q1 Spend | 2,650,000 |
| Variance | -150,000 (-5.4%) |

**Justification:** The Q1 recruitment drive for 12 specialist roles was delayed by four weeks due to
approval process changes under new UAE Federal HR regulations. AED 180,000 in recruitment agency
fees and onboarding costs has been deferred to Q2 2026.

---

### Infrastructure & Facilities (INF)

| Item | Amount (AED) |
|---|---|
| Approved Q1 Budget | 8,500,000 |
| Actual Q1 Spend | 9,750,000 |
| Variance | +1,250,000 (+14.7%) |

**Breakdown of overspend:**

| Expenditure Line | Budgeted | Actual | Variance |
|---|---|---|---|
| Al Quoz Data Centre — Emergency Roof Repair | 0 | 800,000 | +800,000 |
| HVAC System Replacement (server room) | 0 | 450,000 | +450,000 |
| Routine Facilities Maintenance | 3,000,000 | 3,000,000 | 0 |
| Utilities & Lease | 5,500,000 | 5,500,000 | 0 |

**Justification:** Following a structural inspection in January 2026, the Al Quoz data centre
roof was found to require emergency repair to prevent water ingress risk to server infrastructure.
Works were completed in February. Simultaneously, the primary HVAC unit serving the server room
reached end-of-life and required immediate replacement to maintain data centre operating temperature.
Both items classified as emergency capital expenditure and approved by COO.

## Notes & Caveats

- All figures are unaudited and subject to year-end audit adjustment.
- Figures prepared in accordance with EDA Chart of Accounts v3.2.
- FIN department variance of +30.6% exceeds the 25% Board notification threshold per EDA-FIN-POL-003.
- Emergency expenditure items (IT cybersecurity, INF data centre) have been separately documented
  and approved under EDA Emergency Procurement Procedure EDA-PRO-014.
- Deferred expenditure from Q1 to Q2 totals an estimated AED 430,000 across HR and COM.
