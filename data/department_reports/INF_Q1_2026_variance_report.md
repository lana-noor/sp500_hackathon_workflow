## Executive Summary

## Detailed Justification

### 1. Energy & Utilities (+AED 350,000)

**Primary Cause:** Extreme weather conditions and increased data center cooling requirements.

**Details:**
- January-March 2026 temperatures 15% higher than 10-year average
- Data center cooling costs increased +42% due to Zero Trust infrastructure expansion
- Electricity rates increased 8% (government tariff adjustment January 2026)

**Justification:**
The IT Department's Zero Trust implementation significantly increased server rack density in our data center, requiring enhanced cooling. This was not communicated during budget planning. Additionally, unseasonably high temperatures drove cooling costs beyond projections.

**Supporting Evidence:**
- Utility bills (January-March 2026)
- Data center temperature logs showing increased load
- National weather bureau reports

---

### 2. Facility Upgrades (+AED 300,000)

**Primary Cause:** Emergency elevator repair and access control system upgrade.

**Details:**
- Main elevator failure (January 15): Emergency repair +AED 180,000
- Access control system upgrade for security compliance: +AED 120,000

**Justification:**
The main elevator in Headquarters Building suffered a critical mechanical failure, creating accessibility issues and HSE risks. Emergency repair was required. The access control upgrade was mandated by the National Security Authority following a government-wide security review.

---

### 3. Security Systems (-AED 88,000) — UNDERSPEND

**Cause:** Delayed installation of biometric scanners (vendor supply chain issues).

**Impact:** No security risk - legacy card systems remain operational.
